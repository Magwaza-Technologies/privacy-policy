# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the **Soccer AI Predictor** application (the "App") for Android devices. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

## 1. Information We Collect and Use

The App is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal identifying information (such as your name, email, or phone number) to use the core features of the App. 

However, to provide and improve the Service, the App may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to:
*   **Device Information:** Type of mobile device, unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and diagnostic data (such as crash logs).
*   **Local Storage:** Your app settings, saved matches, and prediction history are stored locally on your device's internal storage and are not transmitted to any external servers we control.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, primarily for fetching sports data and displaying advertisements.

*   **Sports Data APIs:** The App connects to external sports APIs to retrieve live scores, fixtures, and statistics. We do not send your personal data to these APIs.
*   **Google AdMob (Advertising):** The App may use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use advertising IDs from your device to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences. You can learn more about how Google uses data for advertising [here](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).
*   **Google Play Services:** The App utilizes Google Play Services, which has its own privacy policy.

Link to privacy policies of third-party service providers used by the app:
*   [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## 3. Security

We value your trust in using our App. All sports data is retrieved over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS). However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.
