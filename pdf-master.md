# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026

Magwaza Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") operates the PDF Master mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Data Collection and Use
PDF Master is built with a privacy-first approach. We do not require user registration or collect personally identifiable information (PII).

**Local Processing:**
Most core functionalities—including camera scanning, image to PDF conversion, local OCR, merging, and editing—operate entirely offline. Your documents and images are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

**Cloud Features (AI Converters):**
Specific advanced conversion tools (e.g., Word to PDF, Excel to PDF) may require temporary cloud processing. If utilized, your files are securely transmitted, processed in real-time, and strictly deleted immediately after processing. We do not retain, analyze, or share your files.

## 2. App Permissions
To provide functionality, the App requires specific device permissions:
*   **Camera:** To capture physical documents using the Camera Scanner feature.
*   **Storage / Photos / Media:** To read and write PDF documents and select images for conversion.

These permissions are used exclusively for app features and are never used to track you or collect data.

## 3. Analytics and Third-Party Services
We do not integrate third-party trackers, adware, or data-mining SDKs. 

## 4. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us with your information. While no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your files.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
